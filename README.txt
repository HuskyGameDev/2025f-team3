CrossCut

CrossCut is a tower defense game with a twist. During waves, you are placed onto the map to fight enemies alongside your towers to defend your objective.

Controls:
WASD: Move
Space: Jump
Left mouse button: Place towers (top-down mode) or attack (first-person mode)
Right mouse button: Sell towers
Esc: Pause
